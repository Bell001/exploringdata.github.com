Evolution of Internet Users from 1990 - 2011

An animated data visualization showing the percentage of Internet users in relation to the Gross Domestic Product (GDP) per capita for the worlds countries from 1990 to 2011.

This visualization is based on Mike Bostock's recreation of Gapminder's The Wealth and Health of Nations made famous by Hans Rosling in his inspiring TED talk.

The data is retrieved from the World Bank's World Development Indicators and rendered using the JavaScript library D3.
