Info

  • How npm Packages are Linked by Dependencies

    2 Interactive graph visualizations that show how npm packages are linked by dependency relations, the smaller one is limited to packages with at least 10 dependent packages. Graphs were pre-processed with Gephi and are rendered with sigma.js.

  • How Participants of the Visualisingdata Census are Connected on Twitter

    The visualisingdata census network graph shows the Twitter connections of the participants of the 1st data visualization census initiated by Andy Kirk.

  • How Footballers Relate based on Google Searches

    The footballers search relations visualization shows a network graph of former and current football players and managers related by Google searches with results containing knowledge graph information.

  • How Programmers Relate based on Google Searches

    The programmers search relations visualization shows a network graph of programmers related by Google searches with results containing knowledge graph information.

  • A Map of Bike Routes in Berlin, Germany

    The bike routes map for Berlin, Germany shows 14 different biking routes through and around the city. Created with OpenLayers, Open Street Map and data from Senatsverwaltung für Stadtentwicklung und Umwelt.

  • Who Voted for Whom at Ballon d'Or 2012

    The Ballon d'Or 2012 visualization shows an interactive network graph of voters and voted players related based on votes cast for the male FIFA Ballon d'Or award in 2012.

  • Relations of James Bond Film Actors based on Common Appearance

    The James Bond Actors visualization is an interactive network graph showing the connections of actors who appeared together in 23 James Bond movies including the latest in the series called Skyfall.

  • A Network Graph of Human Diseases

    The human disease network graph is a visualization based on a study that explored relations of diseases and human genes by Goh K-I, Cusick ME, Valle D, Childs B, Vidal M, Barabási A-L (2007).

  • How Programming Languages Influenced Each Other

    The programming languages influence network is an interactive graph visualization where languages are connected by influence relations based on data from Freebase.
