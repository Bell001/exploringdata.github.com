About Exploring Data

Exploring Data is about mining and visualizing public data with open source tools created by Ramiro Gómez. The topics covered in the visualizations and maps include human development, climate change, programming, software, social networks and football.

To stay informed about new publications and updates you can subscribe to the RSS feed or follow me on Twitter. Also see what other people have said about Exploring Data.
